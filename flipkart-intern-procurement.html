Flipforward Intern Procurement, Bangalore

Flipforward : At Flipkart, we value diverse perspectives and experiences. Flip forward is our initiative to let experienced women professionals currently on a career break & looking for opportunities to re-enter the workforce to begin their second innings at work. It is a six-month program for women returnees that offers impactful projects across multiple domains and functions and is open to any woman who has taken a career break of anywhere between six months to two years. We are committed to enabling a smooth landing for talented women in their second career ecosystem through structured mentorship, on-the-job learning and flexible work options. Whether it leads to a full-time career, or serves to sharpen the skills necessary to take the next step, the Flip Forward Program is a valuable experience for women to continue their quest for excellence. About the role

Commercial team is the central function that procures all materials and services required for use by Flipkart. Commercial team’s mission is to enable internal stakeholders procure suitable materials and services with requisite quality, at an optimal cost, and in a timeline aligned with business goals. Commercial team's objective is to partner with and enable internal customers on their procurement needs. The engagement spans across the process of sourcing, supplier selection, negotiation, contracting, process of Purchase Requisition (PR) to Purchase Order (PO). Some of the areas of procurement which is managed by team are mentioned below:

  • Logistics procurement
  • Marketing procurement
  • Infra, facilities and automation
  • Contract & Services
  • Technology & Telecom
  • Packaging

What you'll do

  • Draft next level process / SOPs for Procurement team
  • Create education nuggets / communication mechanism, for educating stakeholders on procurement process.
  • Execute the stakeholder education program
  • Participate in fresh Procurement Policy draft and roll out.

Skills Required

Communication, Analytical skills, Problem Solving, Influencing skills

Education/Qualification

BSc / BE / MBA / MCA

Desirable Skills

Experience in having created process documents, and if possible, in roll out of process, stakeholder education

Years of Exp.

4 to 7 years
