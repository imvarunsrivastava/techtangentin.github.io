Internship Opportunity (Data Scientists) at HP, Bangalore

The Machine Learning India team at HP PPS R&D is growing. We are hiring talented individuals with expertise in the areas of Recommender Systems, Bayesian Optimisation, Deep Learning on Graphs, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, Time Series and so on. We have a strong focus on research and actively collaborate with academics on open-ended research problems. This internship will help you learn techniques required to build cutting edge deep learning models and deploying them in the real world. Your work here will have a significant impact on HP products, and you will gain a tremendous amount of Knowledgeledge in Machine Learning and Deep Learning. The Hewlett-Packard Company, commonly shortened to Hewlett-Packard or HP, was an American multinational information technology company headquartered in Palo Alto, California, that developed and provided a wide variety of hardware components, as well as software and related services to consumers, small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) and large enterprises, including customers in the government, health and education sectors. Applications are invited for the Internship Opportunity (Data Scientists) at HP, Bangalore.

Qualifications

A Bachelors, Masters, PhD and/or Post Doc in CS, Machine Learning and/or related fields Programming languages: Python, R, Matlab ML frameworks: Keras, Sklearn/H2O, Tensorflow/Pytorch NLP concepts such as information extraction, word embeddings, Sequence Modelling, Transformer Computer vision concepts such as image segmentation using deep learning Solid understanding of the Machine Learning Algorithms Knowledge of RNN, LSTM, and other neural network architectures Strong communication and data presentation/visualization skills Knowledge of the latest and state of the art ML technology

Responsibilities

Use machine learning, data mining and statistical techniques to create new, scalable solutions for business problems on time series, NLP and image data. Design and develop cutting edge algorithms and techniques for NLP, time series and Image. Deploy models in real-world scenarios. Research and implement novel machine learning, deep learning and statistical approaches.
